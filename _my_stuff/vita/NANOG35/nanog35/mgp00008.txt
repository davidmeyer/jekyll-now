
Goals and Objectives

The Objective of this BOF is two-fold

To provide context for the ongoing discussion of the evolution of technical aspects multi-homing in the Internet
And of course, its consequences

Provide additional opportunity for the operational community to provide feedback to the IETF on the direction the IETF is taking with respect to multi-homing in IPv6

Finally, note that while this session is a bit of an experiment, the Best Way to get your input into the IETF is to participate in the IETF WGs
In this case, the shim6 WG
