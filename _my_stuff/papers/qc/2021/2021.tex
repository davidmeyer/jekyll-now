\noindent
Did you know that 2021 is the smallest natural number that can be expressed as the product of two consecutive primes that 
produces a palindrome when multiplied by its reverse and that is a position in $\pi, e$ and $\phi$ with the same digit?

\Huge{
\begin{equation*}
\begin{array}{llll} 
2021 &=& 43 \times 47 \\
2021 \times 1202 &=& 2429242 \\
\pi_{\scriptscriptstyle 2021} = e_{\scriptscriptstyle 2021} = \phi_{\scriptscriptstyle 2021} &=& 3
\end{array}
\end{equation*}}
